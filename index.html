Welcome!

I'm Stephen Tao: UX Designer, performer, marketer.

I'm an interdisciplinary designer with the consumer knowledge and creative mindset to design engaging interfaces.

I'm a sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in business administration with an intended additional major in interdisciplinary human-computer interaction. Currently, I'm looking for a UX Design role. My past experiences in marketing gave me new perspectives on consumer behavior, which led me to the world of HCI. As I discovered the creative applications of my theatrical and musical passions, I knew that UX would be the perfect opportunity for me to explore, iterate on, and solve problems through design.

Feel free to take a look at some of my projects below and/or contact me if you'd like to chat about HCI and UX design!
