About

My name is Stephen Tao and I'm a sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in business administration with an intended additional major in interdisciplinary human-computer interaction. Currently, I'm looking to dive into a UX Design role.

I discovered my passion for UX design from my business endeavors. I was really interested in marketing; more particularly, the aspects of consumer behavior. This was how I transitioned into one of my first projects: Meranti, a marketing management platform for makers. I quickly became enchanted by HCI and now appreciate the importance of functional, aestetic, seamless design.

Regardless, I still love to pursue my creative interests. I'm a comedic improviser, a beatboxer, theatrical performer, musician, and more. Check out some of my clubs/interests below to learn more about me!
